THE UNIVERSE OF ELEMENTARA

The legend begins

The Fracture

Millennia ago, the world of Elementara was governed by three primordial forces in perfect balance: Lightning, Fire, and Ice. These elements were embodied by Ancient Guardians - legendary creatures of unimaginable power.

But a cataclysmic event called "The Fracture" shattered this balance. The Guardians were fragmented into thousands of mystical cards, scattered across dimensions.

Today, you are a Summoner - one of the rare chosen ones capable of channeling the power of these cards. Your mission: collect the rarest cards, rebuild the Guardians, and restore Elementara's balance.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know

How do I get my first cards? ‚ñº

Every new player receives a free Starter Pack with 10 random cards (guaranteed at least 1 Rare). You can then purchase more cards from the Market or buy Card Packs. Complete daily quests to earn tokens and expand your collection!

How does the marketplace work? ‚ñº

The Market is a player-driven economy. You can buy cards from other players or list your own cards for sale. We take a 5% commission on each transaction. Rare cards can be traded, sold, or kept in your collection. All transactions are secure and instant.

Can I sell my cards? ‚ñº

Yes! You own your cards completely. Go to "My Deck", select any card, and click "Sell". Set your price and list it on the Market. Once another player buys it, you receive tokens instantly (minus 5% platform fee). The rarer the card, the higher you can price it!
