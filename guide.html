THE UNIVERSE OF ELEMENTARA

The legend begins

The Fracture

Millennia ago, the world of Elementara was governed by three primordial forces in perfect balance: Lightning, Fire, and Ice. These elements were embodied by Ancient Guardians - legendary creatures of unimaginable power.

But a cataclysmic event called "The Fracture" shattered this balance. The Guardians were fragmented into thousands of mystical cards, scattered across dimensions.

Today, you are a Summoner - one of the rare chosen ones capable of channeling the power of these cards. Your mission: collect the rarest cards, rebuild the Guardians, and restore Elementara's balance.
